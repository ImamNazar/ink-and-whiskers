— the play studio

get your hands
messy.

no rules, no talent required, nothing to buy. four small ways to fall a little bit in love with paint — the same way thabu did.

01 pour a hexagon 02 colour of your day 03 the prompt jar 04 which painting are you?

— activity 01

pour your own hexagon

this is (almost) how a real acrylic pour feels: you drop colour, the colour decides the rest. tap or drag on the canvas to drop paint, pick a palette, then swirl it with the comb. no two pours ever match.

tap anywhere to drop your first colour ↓

her palettes
tools

— pours you "keep" download as an image. stick it on your fridge.

— activity 02

the colour of your day

thabu says every day has a colour. pick the word that's closest to how today feels, and get a little palette to prove it. tap any swatch to copy it — painters welcome.

pick a feeling and i'll mix it for you →

— activity 03

the prompt jar

blank pages are scary; little slips of paper are not. pull one, and paint (or doodle, or photograph) whatever it says. there are no wrong answers in this jar.

— activity 04

which painting are you?

five small questions, zero science, one painting that is secretly about you. answer with your gut.
